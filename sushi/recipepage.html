Things You Need.

  • Sushi Rice (Sushi-Meshi)
  • Japanese rice vinegar (Komezu)
  • Kombu (This is meant for flavoring, not eating)
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fish
  • Bluefin Tuna (Maguro)
  • Salmon (Sake)
  • Japanese amberjack or yellowtail (Hamachi)
  • Mackerel
  • Albacore tuna (Bontoro)
  • Vegetables

Time to make Sushi!

  1. Lay 1 piece of nori onto a bamboo mat.
  2. Layer ½ to 1 cup (100-200 g) of sushi rice over the nori sheet.
  3. Arrange your ingredients lengthwise on top of the rice
    To add some kick to your roll, try including ¼ tsp (1.25 g)
    of wasabi as a filling in your roll.
  4. Roll up the bamboo mat so only 0.25 to 1 inch (0.64 to 2.54 cm) of
    nori is visible.
  5. Squeeze the top and sides of the bamboo mat to form the sushi roll.
  6. Unroll the bamboo and remove the sushi.
  7. Slice the sushi into 6 equally-sized pieces.
